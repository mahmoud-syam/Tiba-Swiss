About Us

We have several branches around the world

Thebes Swiss Company is an international contracting and general trading company headquartered in Biel / Bien, Switzerland

Thebes Swiss Contracting and General Trading Company was established in 2004 in Egypt, in 2016 in Switzerland, in 2020 in Gambia, and in 2021 in Dubai.

We have several branches around the world to serve its customers and facilitate the buying and selling process and the media.
